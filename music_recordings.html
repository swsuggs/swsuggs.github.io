Sterling Suggs ML projects Music Resume

Recordings

I wanted to collect some recordings of my playing, however imperfect, for posterity.
Bach - Liebster Jesu, Wir Sind Hier - BWV 731

One of two chorale preludes that Bach wrote on this hymn tune. The title translates to "Dearest Jesus, we are here".

Bach - Fugue in G minor - BWV 578

I printed the music for this five years ago as my long-range organ goal. It's lots of fun to play, now that I can finally play it!

Come, come ye saints -- Arr. Elliott

At this time, Richard Elliott's improvisation on this hymn was not commercially available, so I transcribed it manually from youtube videos.
